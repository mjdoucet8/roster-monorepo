# Roster

Roster is a sports networking application for athletes and coaches.

The product is intended to help athletes build credible profiles, connect with coaches, discover opportunities, and stay organized around their teams. For coaches, Roster will provide tools to evaluate athletes, manage team information, coordinate communication, and keep recruiting or roster-building workflows in one place.

## Vision

Roster sits at the intersection of sports networking and team operations. The long-term goal is to make it easier for athletes and coaches to find each other, understand fit, and manage the practical work that follows.

Core product themes:

- Athlete profiles that communicate experience, skills, availability, and goals.
- Coach and organization profiles that make programs easier to discover and evaluate.
- Search and networking tools for connecting athletes, coaches, teams, and opportunities.
- Team organization features for rosters, schedules, communication, and shared planning.
- Workflows that support recruiting, tryouts, training groups, and ongoing team management.
